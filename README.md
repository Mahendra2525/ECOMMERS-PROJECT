### Ecommerce Sales Data Analysis 
#### Project Description
This project presents an interactive dashboard to analyze ecommerce sales data. It helps identify trends, 
customer behavior, and business performanceusing visual analytics.

#### Objectives
1.Analyze sales performance over time
2.Understand customer demographics
3.Evaluate impact of discounts
4.Identify top-performing products and regions

#### Key Metrics (Top Cards)
Customers: 29K
Transactions: 55K
Average Sales: 3.01K
Total Sales: 165.71M
Avg Discount: ₹136.99
👉 Insight:
High transactions compared to customers → repeat purchases (good retention).
Strong total revenue with moderate average order value.

#### 👥 Sales by Age Group

25–45: Highest contribution

18–25: Second largest

45–60: Moderate

Under 18 & 60+: Minimal

👉 Insight:

Core target audience = young to middle-aged adults

Marketing should focus on 18–45 segment

#### 📅 Sales by Year

Sales trend increasing from 2020 → 2024

Peaks around 4.0M, dips around 1.7M

👉 Insight:

Overall growth trend

Some seasonal fluctuations → possible festival or campaign-driven spikes

#### 🏷️ Sales by Discount Type

New Year: 83M (dominates)

Others (Seasonal, Save10, Festive, Welcome): much lower

👉 Insight:

Heavy reliance on New Year promotions

Opportunity to improve performance of other campaigns

#### 🚻 Sales by Gender

Male: ~56.1M

Female: ~55.2M

Other: ~54.3M

👉 Insight:

Very balanced distribution → no strong gender bias

Broad appeal across all groups

#### 💸 Discount Usage

Yes: ~83M

No: ~82M

👉 Insight:

Almost 50-50 split

Discounts influence sales, but not overwhelmingly

#### 🌍 Sales by Location

Concentrated across India (major cities)

👉 Insight:

Strong urban presence

Potential to expand into tier-2/tier-3 cities

#### 🛍️ Product Category Sales

Electronics: 49.74M (highest)

Clothing: 32.73M

Beauty & Health: 25.32M

Others: smaller contributions

👉 Insight:

Electronics is the top-performing category

Long-tail categories need optimization

#### 🧠 2. Key Business Insights

📈 Business is growing steadily year over year

🎯 Target audience = 18–45 age group

🛒 Electronics dominates revenue

🎉 Seasonal campaigns (especially New Year) drive huge spikes

⚖️ Balanced gender participation

💰 Discounts help but are not the only driver
